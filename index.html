Developer Student Club

The Google Developer Student Club at Chitkara University is an open for all club for the advancement and promotion of Mobile-first development. The Core team at the DSC will be responsible for the organization of Google tech and open source related events at the university campus. For the year 2017-18, we will be focusing on sharpening the Mobile and web development skills for university students. Various Android and web related workshops will be organized by the DSC at the campus.

Overview
Learn about the best in Mobile Technologies to build for the Next Billion Internet users!

It is no secret that every day, more and more people are accessing the internet for the first time, both in India, and around the world. Statistics show that almost all of these people access the internet via mobile, and 70% of them are mobile ONLY. Judging by that, it is astonishing that as much as 65% developers in India do NOT target Mobile at all! For the next billion users, this needs to change, and this change can come with you, the next generation of developers, making apps and websites keeping Mobile First. At Mobile Developer Day, we aim to have something for everyone, if you're a web developer, check out the Progressive Web Apps and for the ever so awesome Android Developers, we have an awesome session on Monetizing your Apps using Google Admob!
